Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make it spin faster?
Click or tap rapidly to add speed. Use the Boost button for maximum velocity. The faster you click, the higher the RPM. Try flicking quickly for the best results.
Are fidget spinners good for anxiety?
Many people find repetitive spinning motions calming and helpful for focus. Fidget toys can help redirect nervous energy and provide sensory stimulation. While not a medical treatment, many people with ADHD or anxiety find them helpful.
How long will it keep spinning?
The spinner slows down gradually using realistic friction physics, just like a real fidget spinner. Higher speeds last longer before the spin decays. A big boost can keep it going for 30+ seconds.
Does this work on mobile?
Yes. Tap or swipe on the spinner to give it speed. The touch controls work on any smartphone or tablet.
